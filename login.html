Meet Smarter

What is Meet Smarter?

Meet Smarter helps you schedule meet ups with classmates and co-workers.

Not sure where to meet?

  • Use Meet Smarter's map functionality to schedule a Meet Up at a local coffee shop.

Need in person help?

  • Use Meet Smarter to view upcoming meets ups among your cohort.

Want to invite others to your meet up?

  • Meet Smarter's slack integration alerts your cohort when you create an event. No need to send out invites!

Not sure if should you schedule time at the coffee place with only outdoor seating?

  • Meet Smarter's weather integration helps ensure your meet up doesn't get rained out!
